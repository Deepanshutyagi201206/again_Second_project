Job Description

Our team is looking for a Front-end Dev intern with experiance on front end interfaces. You will help define the system architecture, own and implement specific components, and help shape the overall experiance. You must be comfortable working as part of a team while taking the initiative to take lead on new innovations and projects.The ideal candidate will be responsible for designing, developing, testing, and debugging responsive web and mobile applications for the company and also work in coordination with cross-functional teams to design, develop and maintain our next generation websites and web tools. Using JavaScript, HTML and CSS, this candidate will be able to translate user business needs into functional frontend design.
